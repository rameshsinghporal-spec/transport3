Refund Policy

We provide assistance services for vehicle-related applications and transport bookings.

1. Service Charges

All service charges paid are for processing and assistance. These charges are non-refundable once the service process has been initiated.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may only be considered if the service has not been started or processed from our end.

3. Processing Time

If approved, refunds will be processed within 5–7 working days.

4. Non-Refundable Cases

No refund will be provided once the application is submitted or processed.

5. Contact

For refund requests, contact us at: support@transportnumplate.com
