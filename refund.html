Refund Policy

We provide independent assistance services for vehicle-related applications, including guidance, documentation support, and online form processing. Please note that we are a third-party service provider and not affiliated with any government authority.

1. Service Charges

All payments made on this website are for service and assistance charges. These charges cover the cost of providing support, documentation help, and processing guidance. Once the service process has been initiated, these charges become non-refundable.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refund requests may be considered only in cases where the service has not been started or no processing activity has been initiated from our side. Users must submit a refund request within a reasonable time after making the payment.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

No refund will be provided once the application has been submitted, processed, or forwarded for further action. Additionally, delays or rejections caused by external authorities or incorrect user information are not eligible for refunds.

4. Processing Time

If a refund request is approved after review, the amount will be processed within 5–7 working days to the original payment method. Processing time may vary depending on banking or payment gateway procedures.

5. Contact for Refunds

For any refund-related queries or requests, users can contact our support team at: contact@transportservice.com. Please include your transaction details for faster processing.
